About MindHaven

Your campus sanctuary for mental wellness

Our Mission

To provide every university student with a safe, private, and accessible space to support their mental health journey.

Our Story

MindHaven was born from a simple observation: university students face immense pressure but often lack access to timely mental health support. Long wait times, stigma, and limited resources leave many students struggling alone.

We created MindHaven to bridge this gap - offering immediate, anonymous support whenever and wherever students need it.

Our Values
  • Privacy First - Your data is always encrypted and anonymous
  • Compassionate - Every interaction is nurturing and supportive
  • Accessible - Free for all students, available 24/7
  • Evidence-Based - Resources grounded in research
