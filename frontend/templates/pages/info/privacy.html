Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2025

🔒
TL;DR: Your privacy is our top priority. We never sell your data, and all personal information is encrypted.
1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Account information (matric number, email)
  • Chat conversations (anonymized for training)
  • Mood tracking data
  • Assessment responses
2. How We Protect Your Data
  • End-to-end encryption for all chats
  • Anonymous student IDs - we never store your name with your data
  • Regular security audits
  • GDPR and institutional compliance
3. Data Sharing

We only share data in these limited circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • For crisis intervention (if you're in danger)
  • Anonymized data for research (you can opt out)
4. Your Rights
  • Access your data anytime
  • Delete your account and all data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of research
