Why We Started MindHaven

As university students ourselves, we experienced firsthand the mental health crisis on campuses. Long waiting lists for counseling, stigma around seeking help, and the feeling of being alone in our struggles.

We built MindHaven to be the support system we wished we had - something immediate, anonymous, and always available. A place where any student could talk openly without fear.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of students across multiple universities, providing a safe space for mental wellness.
