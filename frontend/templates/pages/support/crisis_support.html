💚 When You're Feeling Overwhelmed

  • ✓ Breathe deeply - Inhale for 4, hold for 4, exhale for 4
  • ✓ Ground yourself - Name 5 things you see, 4 you can touch
  • ✓ Reach out - Call a trusted friend or family member
  • ✓ Move your body - Take a short walk or stretch
  • ✓ Stay hydrated - Drink a glass of water
  • ✓ Use a coping skill - Try the 5-4-3-2-1 technique
